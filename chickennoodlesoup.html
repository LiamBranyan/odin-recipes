Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Chicken Stock
  • 1 Cup of Chopped Carrots
  • 6oz Cooked Pasta
  • Pepper

    • Cooking Instructions

    1. Bring Stock to a Simmer
    2. Sear chicken Breasts Until Fully Cooked
    3. Bring pasta to a boil and cook until al dente
    4. Add all ingredients to stock
    5. allow all ingredients to simmer until fully marinated together
    6. Enjoy
