Scampi Fra Diavolo

Description

Scampi Fra Diavolo is a delicious dish that has a little bit of a spice to it as well as a sweet tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Shrimp
  • Chili Pepper
  • linguine

Cooking Instructions

  1. Bring Water to a boil
  2. Salt the boiling water
  3. Add the linguine to the pot
  4. sear shrimp in a pan after seasoning
  5. put butter and chili into the pan as the shrimp cook
  6. reduce the pan to a simmer
  7. add crushed tomatos into the pan with shrimp
  8. finally combine all ingredients into a large pot ready to serve
