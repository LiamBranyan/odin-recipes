Shrimp Spring Rolls

Description

These Shrimp Spring rolls are very easy to make as you are good at working with rice paper.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Rice Paper
  • Lettuce
  • rice noodles
  • Carrots
  • Chili Sauce
  • Peanut Sauce

Instructions

  1. First start buy soaking 2 pieces of rice paper at a time in a medium plate
  2. Boil your shrimp
  3. chop your lettuce
  4. once your lettuce is dry start cutting it into leafy pieces

    5. *Assemle on top of rice paper in following order

  5. Lettuce
  6. shrimp
  7. rice noodles
  8. carrots
  9. wrap rice paper like it is a burrito
  10. Dip in your favorite sauce and enjoy
